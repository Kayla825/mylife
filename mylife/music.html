Favorite Music

dj-jo/Zenpaku

Dj Jo (or Zenpaku) is a VOCALOID producer who usually makes EDM remixes of video game music or anime music. He's probably one of my favorite artists because puts a twist on music I usually listen to.


Big Bang

Big Bang is a Korean group of 5 members. I like this particular group because most of their songs usually involve loud drums and minor keys. In general, I just think that their songs are catchy even though it's all in Korean.
